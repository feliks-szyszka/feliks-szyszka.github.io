Hi. I'm Feliks. I design, engineer and manufacture embedded systems / electronic products in my spare time. It all started with an Ender 3 Pro 3D Printer, a soldering iron and a bit too much curiosity, which quickly became a deep desire to learn all the disciplines required to have the freedom to build whatever I wanted.

I believe the best products come from treating engineering, design, UX, and UI as one intertwined collection of skills rather than separate disciplines. At the end of the day a well-routed PCB is nothing without software, or components for that matter.

That's why I focus on building things that are pleasant to use, easy to assemble and repair, and provide the end user with excellent value... as Dieter Rams said: "less, but better".
